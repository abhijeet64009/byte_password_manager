Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/11/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Abhijeet Kumar (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use this mobile application (“the App”).

This App functions completely offline and does not collect, store, or share personal data with any server, cloud service, or third party. All sensitive information is stored locally on your device and secured using AES encryption.

If you use the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you.

The App operates fully offline and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share information with third parties
  • Track your device, location, or usage
  • Connect to the internet for data purposes

2. Sensitive Data You Store in the App

The App allows you to store passwords, notes, card details, and more. All such data is:

  • Encrypted locally using AES
  • Never uploaded to any cloud or server
  • Accessible only on your device

We cannot access or decrypt any information stored in the App.

3. Encryption and Security

All data is protected using industry-standard AES encryption:

  • Only you can access your stored information
  • Your data is never transmitted online
  • Your information remains safe even offline

If you uninstall the App or delete its storage, all encrypted data is permanently erased.

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer subscriptions or one-time unlocks for premium features. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not process or store payment details.

5. Exporting Data

The App allows exporting data as an AES-encrypted JSON file. This file:

  • Remains encrypted
  • Can only be decrypted inside the App
  • Is not shared with us or third parties

You are responsible for keeping the exported file safe.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics, ads, cloud backups, or external databases.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

We may update this policy occasionally. Any updates will appear on this page.
