Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/11/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Abhijeet Kumar (“we”, “our”) handles information inside the Byte Password Manager app.

1. No Data Collected

Byte Password Manager collects zero data. Everything you store remains fully offline and encrypted on your device.

2. What You Store

You may store passwords, notes, card details or secure entries. All content stays only on your device.

3. AES Encryption

All data is encrypted using AES. You can export your vault as an encrypted JSON file for offline backup.

4. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled securely by Google Play. We do not receive or store payment data.
