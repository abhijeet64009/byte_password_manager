Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/11/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Abhijeet Kumar handles information when you use the Byte Password Manager application.

1. Offline-first & Local Storage

All user data is stored locally on the device and encrypted with AES. We do not upload your passwords or notes to any server.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data is collected or transmitted.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.

3. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled by Google Play Billing. We do not process or store payment details.

4. Export & Backup

You can export your data as an AES-encrypted JSON file. You are responsible for storing that file securely.

5. Security

Your data is protected by AES. If you uninstall the app or clear storage, encrypted data is removed from your device.
