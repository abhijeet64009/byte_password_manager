Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/11/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Abhijeet Kumar (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use this mobile application (“the App”).

This App is designed to function completely offline and does not collect, store, or share personal data with any server, cloud service, or third party. All sensitive information saved by the user is stored locally on their device and protected using AES encryption.

If you use the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you.

The App operates fully offline and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share information with third parties
  • Track your device, location, or usage
  • Connect to the internet for data purposes

2. Sensitive Data You Store in the App

The App allows you to store information such as passwords, secure notes, card details, and other private entries. All such data is:

  • Encrypted locally using AES
  • Never uploaded to any cloud or server
  • Accessible only on your device

We cannot access, decrypt, or recover any information stored by you in the App.

3. Encryption and Security

All stored data is encrypted using industry-standard AES encryption methods. This ensures:

  • Only you can access your stored information
  • Your data is never transmitted online
  • Your information remains safe even if your device is offline

If you uninstall the App or delete its storage, all encrypted data is permanently erased.

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases (subscriptions or one-time unlocks) to access premium features. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not process or store your payment details.

5. Exporting Data

The App allows you to export your stored passwords and data as an AES-encrypted JSON file. This exported file:

  • Remains encrypted
  • Can only be decrypted inside the App
  • Is not shared with us or any third party

You are responsible for storing this exported file safely.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services such as analytics, advertising networks, cloud backups, or external databases.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
