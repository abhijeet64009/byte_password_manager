Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/11/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Abhijeet Kumar (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use the Byte Password Manager application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you. The App operates fully offline and does not collect, store, or share personal data with any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. Sensitive Data You Store in the App

The App allows you to store information such as passwords, secure notes, card details, and other private entries. All such data is encrypted locally using AES, never uploaded, and accessible only on your device.

3. Encryption & Export

All stored data is encrypted using AES. You can export your data as an AES-encrypted JSON file and are responsible for storing it securely.

4. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled by Google Play Billing. We do not process or store payment details.
